Welcome to the
B-Wing in Marlboro High School

What is the
B-Wing?

The B-wing was an added portion to the school focusing on 21st century electives. The B-wing is not just home to regular classrooms, as they contain resources and materials that capitalize on real world exposure and allow for an effective learning experience.

What does the
B-Wing offer?

There are specific classes and clubs that are either only offered in the B-Wing or originate from the B-Wing. Most count towards the 21st century requirement, where all offer unforgettable experiences inside and outside the classroom.

Who's the
staff?

The B-Wing is home to many unique, education driven, and responsible teachers. They not only set up students for success, but also connect on a deeper level. This presents students with a trusted adult and a true role model inside the classroom.
